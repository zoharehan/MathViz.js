MathViz.js

A Mathematical Visualisation Library

This library can generate interactive visualisations for a variety of primary-mathematical concepts to help improve student understanding.
It provides a range of customisation options to allow integration into different forms of online-learning platforms and aims to aid in creating more tailored and immersive learning experiences :)

Let's explore some of our features!


Addition & Subtraction Visualisation

You see four dogs at the shelter, Allan, Cook and Murphy and Leo. Allan and Leo get adopted. How many furry friends are left now?

Imagine three kittens were sleeping together in your house. Five more came
and joined them. How many sleeping kittens are in your house now?

Click on the equals sign to see results and click on it again to hide them!
You can also hover over the items to see additional information, in this case, the numbers being represented :)

Note that the description at the bottom as well as the choice between cats and dogs can be customised by the developer.

Interative Fraction Visualisations

You and your friend just bought a pizza with 6 slices. If your friend eats three slices, how much of the pizza has your friend eaten in fraction form? What if the pizza had 7 slices? 8?

Click on slices to see how the fraction changes :)

Note that the developer can customise the total slices, filled slices and fill color!

Matrix-Scalar Multiplication Step-by-Step Visualisations

New to Matrices? Our library's interactive animations makes the familiarisation process easier!

Click on the "Next Step" button to walk through the steps taken to multiply a scalar with a matrix!

Once you finish going through the steps, you'll be informed through an alert.
If you want to go over them again, simply press "OK" and then click on the "Next Step" button to start the visualisation from the beginning!

Note that the developer can customise the scalar, matrix dimensions and values to better suit their task :)

Stumbling on Matrix Multiplication?

Not to worry! Our library's Matrix-Matrix Multiplication Visualiser is here to help and just one simple function call away!

Click on the "Next Step" button to walk through the steps taken to multiply two matrices!
Every click highlights the row and column being multiplied together as well as the result cell they correspond to.

Note that the developer can customise the matrices' dimensions and values to better suit their task :)
